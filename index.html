Mike Dzl

Photography, Video, Slow Motion, Timelapse and Artistic Design

I hail from Ithaca NY and am a true "Digital Nomad" who excels at capturing the feeling of moments and bringing them to life online. This web site serves as a "launch point" for some of my photography, video and digital artwork online. I often shoot live music events, which then get posted to either my Facebook or Instagram I've linked below.

I develop web sites, web applications, digital signage, digital experiences for clients as large as Blues Traveler, George Clinton & Cornell University as well as countless others as my main job for over 20 years now. Hit me up @ xgeneration.net for more information on that side of my brain or if you would like some consultation.
